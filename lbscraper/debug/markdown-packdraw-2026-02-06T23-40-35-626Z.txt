### RULES

Wager abusers will be disqualified from their winnings. Fair play is required to maintain leaderboard integrity.

### HOW IT WORKS

1

#### WAGER

Place bets using your referral code to earn points and climb the leaderboard rankings

2

#### COMPETE

Stay active and maintain your position on the leaderboard to secure bigger rewards

3

#### WIN

Top players receive their rewards automatically at the end of each competition period

### FAQ

#### How are points calculated?

Points are calculated based on your total wager amount during the competition period. The more you wager using your referral code, the more points you earn.

#### When do competitions end?

Competitions run on a weekly basis. They start every Monday at 00:00 UTC and end on Sunday at 23:59 UTC. Rewards are distributed after each weekly competition ends.

#### How are rewards distributed?

Rewards are distributed to your account based on your final ranking. The prize pool is split among the top 10 players, with higher rankings receiving larger shares of the prize pool.

#### Can I participate in multiple competitions?

Yes, you can participate in competitions across different platforms (Roobet, Clash.gg, and Cases.gg) simultaneously. Each platform has its own separate leaderboard and prize pool.
